# Product Surface Gate

Use before stack selection for new products or major product pivots.

## Decide The Surface

- [ ] Website, web app, mobile app, mini program, browser extension, IDE/Codex plugin, desktop app, API/service, or hybrid.
- [ ] Main user entry point and acquisition path.
- [ ] Expected usage frequency and session length.
- [ ] Device and ecosystem constraints.
- [ ] Distribution, review, and release cost.
- [ ] First-version surface and later expansion path.

## Default Heuristics

- Website/web app: low install friction, SEO, broad access, fast MVP.
- Mobile app: high-frequency consumer use, native notifications, camera, location, or offline needs.
- Mini program: WeChat/private-domain flows, lightweight service, social commerce.
- Plugin/extension: work happens inside another tool and context switching is the pain.
- API/service: value is consumed by developers or other systems.

## Approval

The Human Lead must confirm the first-version product surface before scaffolding or stack selection.
